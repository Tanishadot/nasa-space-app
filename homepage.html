The Solar System is a vast and intricate collection of celestial bodies bound together by gravity. At its center lies the Sun, a medium-sized star that provides the energy necessary for life on Earth. The Solar System is located in the Orion Arm of the Milky Way galaxy, approximately 27,000 light-years from the galactic center.
